About Unlac Health

Unlac Health helps real people build healthier lives using simple, evidence-based nutrition and realistic behaviour changes. Our focus is metabolic health, hormonal balance and long-term habit change — not quick fixes. We design personalised plans for PCOD/PCOS, thyroid support, diabetes management & reversal, gut health, weight management and child nutrition.
