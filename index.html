About Unlac Health

Unlac Health was founded to make nutrition clear, achievable and kind. I combine practical meal plans with behaviour support to help people reverse and manage lifestyle conditions — such as PCOD/PCOS, thyroid imbalance, diabetes, gut issues and weight concerns — without extreme measures.

Every plan is personalised: realistic meals, weekly guidance, and slowly built habits that last. We focus on metabolic health, hormone balance, gut wellbeing and sustainable weight management — so clients regain energy, confidence and long-term results.
